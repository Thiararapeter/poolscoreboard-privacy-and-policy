As a data subject, you have comprehensive rights regarding your personal data. We make it easy to exercise these rights.

Right of Access

What it means: You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

What we provide:

  • Confirmation of data processing
  • Copy of your personal data
  • Information about data sources
  • Processing purposes and legal basis

Response time: Within 30 days

Right to Rectification

What it means: You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

When to use:

  • Your name is spelled incorrectly
  • Game data contains errors
  • Any information is outdated

Response time: Within 30 days

üóëÔ∏è

Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten)

What it means: You can request deletion of your personal data under certain circumstances.

When applicable:

  • Data no longer necessary for original purpose
  • You withdraw consent
  • Data processed unlawfully
  • Legal obligation requires deletion

Response time: Immediate for Pool Scoreboard (we auto-delete within 24h anyway!)

‚è∏Ô∏è

Right to Restrict Processing

What it means: You can request we limit how we process your data.

When to use:

  • You contest data accuracy
  • Processing is unlawful but you don't want deletion
  • We no longer need data but you need it for legal claims

Response time: Within 30 days

üì¶

Right to Data Portability

What it means: You can receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

What you get:

  • JSON format export
  • All your game data
  • Player statistics
  • Account information

Response time: Within 30 days

üö´

Right to Object

What it means: You can object to processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing.

When applicable:

  • Processing for legitimate interests
  • Direct marketing (we don't do this!)
  • Scientific/historical research
  • Statistical purposes

Response time: Within 30 days
